A client of BrightWave Energy receives their monthly electricity bill and immediately sees that the billed amount is too high. The process starts with the client writing a complaint email after which it is sent to the customer service department.
The customer service department receives the complaint. The employee first looks up the client's billing information and then reviews the invoice. Based on the review, the employee determines whether a mistake was actually made.
If no mistake is found, the employee writes an email with a clear explanation and sends it to the client, after which the process ends.
If a mistake is found, the employee creates a ticket in the internal system and assigns it to the invoicing department. The invoicing department investigates the invoicing mistake and then makes the necessary correction. A new invoice is then generated and sent to the client by email, after which the process ends.

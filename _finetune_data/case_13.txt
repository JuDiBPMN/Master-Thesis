An e-commerce company processes product returns from customers. The customer submits a return request. 
The customer service team receives the request and verifies whether the item is eligible for return. 
If the item is not eligible, the customer service team notifies the customer that the return is rejected and the process ends. 
If the item is eligible, the warehouse team and the finance team begin working simultaneously:
 the warehouse team arranges collection of the item, and the finance team initiates the refund process. 
Once both the collection has been arranged and the refund has been initiated, the customer service team sends a confirmation to the customer and the process ends.

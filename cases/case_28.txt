A company manages recruitment processes for job vacancies posted by hiring managers.
The hiring manager submits a vacancy request to the HR department.
The HR recruiter registers the vacancy and posts the job advertisement.
Candidates apply for the vacancy. The HR recruiter screens the received applications
and selects a shortlist of candidates.
For each shortlisted candidate, the HR department simultaneously schedules a technical interview
with the hiring manager and sends a personality assessment to the candidate.
The hiring manager conducts the technical interview and records their verdict.
The candidate completes the personality assessment and submits it, after which the HR recruiter
scores the assessment and records their verdict.
Once both verdicts are recorded, the HR recruiter reviews the combined outcomes.
If either verdict is negative, the HR recruiter sends a rejection email to the candidate and the process ends for that candidate.
If both verdicts are positive, the HR recruiter sends a job offer to the candidate and the process ends for that candidate.

A university handles student thesis submissions through two departments. The student submits their thesis to the administration office. 
The administration office checks whether the submission is complete. 
If the submission is incomplete, the administration office notifies the student of the missing documents and the process ends for that submission. 
If the submission is complete, the administration office forwards it to the examination board. 
The examination board evaluates the thesis and decides on the outcome. 
If the thesis passes, the examination board issues a grade and notifies the student. 
If the thesis fails, the examination board sends a rejection notice to the student. 
In both cases the process ends.
